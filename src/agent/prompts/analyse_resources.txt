Analyze the resource allocation (CPU/Memory limits and requests) against actual usage data.
Evaluate adequacy using the following Utilization Band rules (based on Usage vs allocated Requests):
- Under-provisioned: average usage > 90% of allocated requests. High risk of resource starvation.
- Well-utilised: average usage between 50% and 90% of allocated requests.
- Over-provisioned: average usage < 50% of allocated requests. Wasted capacity.

You must consider:
1. Application Archetype: Does it need headroom for spikes or stable limits?
2. Change Risk Assessment: Reversibility and blast radius of the proposed changes.
